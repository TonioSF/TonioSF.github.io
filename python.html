Quiz Challenge

One of the fun things that people can do when they are bored or wanting something productive to do to pass time is to answer to riddles. They can exercise their brain and use their critical thinking. I made a challenge that would randomly bring up three riddles that people can try to answer. There is no score keeping in this challenge, only fun guessing to test their critical thinking.
