The Art Generator by Tonio Wu

Many times when wee are trying to draw something and be productive, we could not think of anything to draw. That is why I thought of making this app. This art Generator will help many artist to have motivations. This app is good for drawing practice as well. Every artists need this kind of app in their daily life to keep themselves motivated each day.
